CREATING LEAD DRILL THROUGH MATRIX 23


To create lead drill through matrix, repeat all the steps in "Step 13"through "Step 15"
with  a few changes.
1. Duplicate the Overview page and rename it as "Lead Drill Through"
2. Delete all visuals
3. Click on Matrix from visualizations pane
4. Drag these columns to the ROWS from hubspot table
"Total",  "Lead_Date" and "Actual Lead Source"

                             


5.  Add the following columns and measures one by one to the Values field
"Average open rate", "Average click rate", "Click rate marketing lead MoM%",
"Enrollment rate", "open rate marketing lead MoM% and "prospect"(lead)
